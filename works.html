JOS 2012 Lab Reports
I have been the TA of "Opearting System Lab (Honer Track)" course of Peking University twice. This course uses same material with MIT 6.828. During the courses it is my job to host weekly seminars, help students finish their labs, and answer questions. To better help students learn this course, I briefly wrote the lab reports.
